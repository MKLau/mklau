I have been working with Dr. Stuart Borrett’s Systems Ecology and Ecoinformatics Lab (aka. the SEELab) at the University of North Carolina Wilmington. to apply and develop computational tools for analyzing complex ecological networks. Together we have produced the enaR Ecosystem Network Analysis (ENA) package that incorporates over 40 years of research into network analysis algorithms.
